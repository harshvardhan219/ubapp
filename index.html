About

UTKARSH BHARAT is an interactive software application developed to utilize the core technologies of the social networking platforms to create an extensive social media and knowledge framework for the nation. Unlike traditional Social Networks, our approach and vision towards the development of this platform focuses on accessibility of rich Indian culture, heritage and values towards the Indian youth and citizens at large.

With the functionalities of this app we want to offer a platform that will have an extensive penetration in India and while minimizing the on-screen time people spend on social networks.

Absolute security

The app is secured with latest encryption algorithms and provides seamless user experience with minimal learning curve. The app's 'Sign Up' process is based on Mobile Verification through OTP and registration can be done under 3 minutes.
